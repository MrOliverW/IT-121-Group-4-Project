    • Tennis

    Tennis has been more than just a game for me; it's been a journey of passion, discovery, and connection. My love for tennis blossomed in high school, where the courts became my sanctuary. It was there that I first felt the thrill of competition and the joy of connecting with others who shared my passion. Tennis taught me the value of hard work, resilience, and sportsmanship. Each match was a battle, not just against my opponent, but against my own limits, pushing me to excel and grow. The high school tennis court was where I forged lasting friendships and learned invaluable life lessons. The sound of the ball hitting the racket, the strategic movements, and the intense focus required in every game made me fall in love with the sport. Competing for the high prize was exhilarating, but what truly captured my heart was the spirit of the game—the respect for opponents, the camaraderie among teammates, and the sheer love of the game.






    Photography

    Photography has always been a passion of mine, but during the unprecedented times of COVID-19, it evolved into something much more significant—a lifeline that connected me to the world outside and brought warmth to the isolation of lockdown. As the world slowed down and people retreated into their homes, I found solace in capturing the stillness and beauty of the world through my lens. Photography became my window to the outside world, allowing me to explore and document the quiet, deserted streets, the resilience of nature, and the myriad of emotions playing out in the eyes of those around me. The pandemic, despite its challenges, opened up new avenues for connection. Sharing my photographs online brought me closer to a community of fellow photography enthusiasts, people who, despite the physical distances and lockdowns, shared a common bond through our love for capturing moments. These connections were a beacon of hope and companionship in a time when social interactions were limited to screens and phone calls. Engaging with this community, exchanging tips, stories, and encouragement, not only improved my skills but also filled many lonely hours with meaningful interactions and learning.






    Gaming

    My love for gaming transcends mere pastime; it's a journey, an adventure, and a testament to perseverance, especially when it comes to the captivating world of "League of Legends." The game isn't just about reflexes and strategies; it's about the stories, the community, and the personal milestones that make every match unforgettable. Among the myriad of achievements that I've treasured over the years, reaching the Emerald tier stands out as a pinnacle of my dedication and passion for this game. The climb to Emerald was no ordinary feat. It was a journey filled with countless battles, each teaching me more about patience, teamwork, and the fine art of strategy. Every match was a new lesson, every opponent a new challenge to overcome. The road to Emerald was paved with both triumphs and setbacks, but it was the setbacks that taught me resilience, urging me to refine my strategies, to adapt, and to come back stronger.






    Reading

    My love for reading is a cherished chapter in the narrative of my life, a journey that has been profoundly enriched by the companionship of books. Within the walls of my home, shelves and tables are graced with an ever-growing collection of over 30 volumes, each holding a world within its pages waiting to be explored. My selection predominantly orbits around the realm of science, a reflection of my academic and personal fascination with the subject. Having majored in a science discipline, my inclination towards scientific literature is not just an extension of my academic pursuits but a testament to my unquenchable thirst for knowledge. Each book in my collection is a gateway to discovery, offering insights into the mysteries of the universe, the intricacies of biology, the principles of physics, and the wonders of chemistry. These books are more than mere sources of information; they are mentors, guiding me through complex concepts and theories, expanding my understanding and fueling my curiosity. The joy of delving into a new book, of turning its pages and absorbing its knowledge, is a pleasure unparalleled.






    Film

    My passion for film is not just about entertainment; it's a cornerstone of my life, a crucial block in the foundation of who I am. Films have been my companions through various chapters, offering a window into different worlds and perspectives. This love affair with cinema began as a simple fascination with stories and visuals, but it has since blossomed into an enduring appreciation for the art of filmmaking. To me, every film is a canvas where directors paint with light, writers sculpt with words, and actors breathe life into characters. The darkened theater is a temple where I commune with emotions, ideas, and questions that stir the soul. Each viewing experience is a journey—an education of the heart and the mind.
